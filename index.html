Research Experience

DISCRETIONARY MINER NOMINATION ALGORITHM FOR SECURED IOTA ENABLED MICRO TRANSACTION

IEEE REGION 10 SYMPOSIUM (TENSYMP)
JUNE 5, 2020

IOTA is a miner-less framework which runs on a different mechanism than Blockchain. The participation of every participant is compulsory to validate the transaction. While validating, low powered devices create a bottleneck situation that increases the average Transaction Per Second (TPS). To solve this, Discretionary Mining Algorithm(DMA) chooses a particular miner who will outsource the computation of the low-end devices and remove the bottleneck situation.
