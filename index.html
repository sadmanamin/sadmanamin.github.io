Research Experience

DISCRETIONARY MINER NOMINATION ALGORITHM FOR SECURED IOTA ENABLED MICRO TRANSACTION

IEEE REGION 10 SYMPOSIUM (TENSYMP)
JUNE 5, 2020

IOTA is a minerless framework which runs on a different than Blockchain. In this platform, there is no miner who will mine the blocks. Every participant of IOTA network need to participate and validate the transaction. While doing this validation, low powered devices create a bottleneck situation which increases the average transaction per second or TPS. To solve this, Discretionary Mining Algorithm comes to choose partial miners who will outsource the computation of the low end devices and remove the bottleneck situation.
